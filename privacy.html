🪔 KrishnaVerse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

KrishnaVerse ("we", "us", "the app") is a Bhagavad Gita study and devotional companion available as a website (krishnaverse.online) and an Android/iOS app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We do not sell your personal data, and the app is ad-free.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account details — your email address and display name, when you create an account (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Your content — journal/reflection entries, bookmarked verses, and your Japa (chant) counts. These are stored so they sync across your devices.
  • Support messages — anything you send us by email or WhatsApp.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data — app version, device/browser type, and error logs, used to keep the service working and fix bugs.
  • Preferences — settings such as language and theme, stored on your device and/or your account.

2. Payments

If you purchase Premium, payment is processed by Razorpay (and the UPI app you choose). We do not receive or store your card number, UPI PIN, or bank credentials. We store only the fact that a payment succeeded (an order ID and your Premium status) so we can unlock your features. Razorpay's handling of payment data is governed by their own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your account, content, and Premium entitlement.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the app.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To send essential account emails (e.g., email verification, password reset).

4. How Your Data Is Stored & Shared

Data is stored using Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Functions), which acts as our data processor. We share data only with service providers that help us run the app — currently Google Firebase (hosting, database, auth) and Razorpay (payments) — and only as needed to provide the service, or where required by law. We never sell your data or use it for advertising.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below; we will delete it within 30 days, except where we must retain limited records (e.g., payment/tax records) by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below.

7. Children

KrishnaVerse is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), authenticated access, and server-side security rules. Premium status can only be granted by our secure server after a verified payment — it cannot be altered from a user's device. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, by an in-app notice.
